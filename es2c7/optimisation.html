Multidimensional Optimisation

Optimisation tasks involve finding x such that S is at an extremum (max/min).

Consider a continuous function S(x), expanded about the point x0​, with a vector ζ as the displacement from x0​:

S(x0​+ζ)≊S(x0​)+(∂x∂S​)′ζ+21​ζ′(∂x2∂2S​)ζ=S(x0​)+gx0​′​ζ+21​ζ′Hx​ζ

If g′(x0​)=0, then:
